My favorite Hobby

Drawing


Drawing is a form of visual art, sometime using just a pencil. Or using other tools such as wax crayon, water paint, e.t.c. Drawing is a form of visual art in which an artist uses instruments to mark paper or other two-dimensional surface. Drawing instruments include graphite pencils, pen and ink, various kinds of paints, inked brushes, colored pencils, crayons, charcoal, chalk, pastels, erasers, markers, styluses, and metals.
